25 October 2014

Redswandir updated:

  • The Big Mining Update is here and done! Blessed be!
  • Map: Removed the mining asteroid, and replaced it with more asteroids. The old one may still be around somewhere...
  • Map: Labor Camp is now in the asteroid field, and just east of the asteroid field beacon is a small mining outpost for miners.
  • Map: Added Space Beacons around the station, for ease of space travel.
  • Map: Fleshed out the Mining Dock, removed the mining shuttle but added in mecha recharge bays for every miner.
  • Every miner now gets a hand tele and a mining jetpack to quickly traverse from the asteroid field to the station.
  • Mining Ore rates now variable, ranging from the current 3 to a new high of 7!
  • Added "new" ores, so get used to seeing lots of Bananium, Mimesteinium, and Adamantine in the asteroid field.
  • Since Mimesteinium, and Adamantine have no real use, you can now sell refined minerals and get points! Things like metal and glass will give you a little amount while plasma, uranium and diamonds more so. If you want to be cruel, mine a bunch of Bananium or Mimesteinium and sell it in stacks of 50 for mad dosh.
  • Big blast doors are still fucked: so toxins has old ones for a little while so you can test bombs without nuking yourself.
  • Some small assorted map changes as well, either for aesthetics or for ease of use: escape arm is now more visible to AIs and there are more air alarms in general, for one example.
  • Increased job limits of miners up to 4, from 3, and of cyborgs up to 3, from 1.
